I'm a graduate student at UChicago applying ML models to extract behavioral signals from large social datasets using text, vision, and multimodal approaches. I completed my undergrad at IIT Kharagpur, where my research focused on statistical learning approaches to behavioral economics.

Here's a sample of my research on personality inference from Twitter. [Open-source release of models on Hugging Face library coming soon!] I've provided a non-technical introduction to my work on Medium.

Over the last few months, I've been building an end-to-end ML pipeline to generate investment signals from of millions of call transcripts using NLP & Deep Learning, for Voya Financial. It incorporates some of the latest advances in Transformer-base models to generate novel behavioral signals from text, by processing billions of tokens of data.

When I'm not performing grid-searches or sampling for drift, I usually like to read non-fiction [esp. behavioral science] and indulge my addiction to aviation and popular science. I also like photography.
